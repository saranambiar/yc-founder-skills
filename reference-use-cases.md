## 1. Should I even build this?

### User knows what to ask

“I’m thinking about building *SyncLoop* — an AI tool that monitors Slack, GitHub, and Linear to automatically detect project drift in software teams.

I researched the space and found several competitors already doing adjacent things. My version is more focused on engineering execution and risk detection specifically.

How do I evaluate whether this is a validated market opportunity versus an overcrowded space I’m entering too late?”

### User doesn’t know what to ask

“I’ve been working on this AI product for engineering teams that automatically figures out when projects are going off track from team activity and conversations.

At first it felt genuinely exciting and original, but after researching the space I found a bunch of companies doing similar things.

Now I can’t tell if I discovered a real market opportunity or if I just reinvented something people already solved better.”
